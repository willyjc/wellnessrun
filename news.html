The Governor

governor’s Endorsement and a Celebration of Community Health and Wellness We are thrilled to announce that the Hopkins Wellness Run is scheduled for November 10th, 2024, and we are honored to have the official endorsement of the county’s esteemed Governor! This significant support from the county leadership is a testament to our shared commitment to community health, wellness, and the value of public-private partnerships. The Hopkins Wellness Run is not just another race; it’s a community movement to promote healthier lifestyles and foster unity among residents. By participating in this event, residents of all ages and fitness levels will have an opportunity to engage in a fun, accessible activity that underscores the importance of physical health as part of an overall wellness routine. Running and walking are powerful ways to combat stress, improve cardiovascular health, and inspire others to embrace an active lifestyle. In a recent meeting, the Governor expressed his excitement about supporting our initiative, highlighting the county government's commitment to collaborating with local organizations and businesses to create a thriving, wellness-oriented community. The Governor noted, “The Hopkins Wellness Run is a wonderful initiative that aligns with our goals of improving the well-being of our residents. We believe that by partnering with private entities, we can achieve more and better serve our community.” His endorsement speaks volumes about the county government’s dedication to fostering a healthy and supportive environment for all. Through the Hopkins Wellness Run, we are building a legacy of health, wellness, and community pride that will resonate long after the event. With the county government’s endorsement, we’re excited to set a new standard for community-driven initiatives that benefit everyone involved.

We invite everyone to come out and show their support for the Paralympic team and to be inspired by their stories of perseverance and triumph. Together, let’s make this year’s Hopkins Wellness Run an unforgettable experience that champions the ideals of strength, unity, and health for all

The Hopkins Hospitals

Hopkins Wellness Run: Raising Awareness of Chronic Conditions Hopkins Hospitals is proud to present the Hopkins Wellness Run, a flagship event aimed at fostering awareness and education around chronic health conditions. Taking place on November 10th, 2024, this run is not just about physical activity—it’s a collective call to action for understanding, preventing, and managing chronic conditions that impact our families, friends, and neighbors every day. With full support from the county’s Governor and a special appearance by Kenya’s Paraplegic Team, the Hopkins Wellness Run stands as a testament to the power of community health initiatives and the importance of working together to improve quality of life. Chronic health conditions, including diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and respiratory illnesses, affect millions worldwide, and our own community is no exception. Many people live with these conditions, yet awareness about their causes, symptoms, and preventive measures remains limited. Through this run, Hopkins Hospitals aims to shine a light on these prevalent issues and emphasize the critical role that early detection, lifestyle changes, and proper management play in reducing the burden of chronic illness. The Hopkins Wellness Run is designed to engage participants and spectators alike in meaningful conversations about their health and well-being, with resource stations, interactive activities, and expert speakers available throughout the event. We invite you, your family, and your friends to join us on November 10th for a day that celebrates health, wellness, and resilience. Whether you’re running, walking, or cheering from the sidelines, your participation helps build a healthier, stronger community. The Hopkins Wellness Run is more than a day of physical activity; it’s a demonstration of our shared commitment to making our county a place where every resident has access to the knowledge and resources needed to lead a fulfilling, healthy life. Come be a part of a movement that goes beyond fitness—it’s a journey towards lasting health for everyone. Let’s come together to make a lasting impact on the health of our community. See you at the starting line!
