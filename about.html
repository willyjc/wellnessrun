The Hopkins Wellness Run

The Hopkins Wellness Run is a community-driven event dedicated to raising awareness about chronic conditions that affect millions globally, including diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, chronic kidney disease, asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), cancer, and obesity. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and resources needed to prevent and manage these conditions, fostering a healthier, more informed community. Through this event, we aim to encourage active lifestyles, promote early detection, and reduce the stigma surrounding chronic illness. Join us in taking steps toward better health and creating a brighter future for those affected by chronic conditions.
